INTRODUCTION.

A four day surfeit of fun activities and ecstasy, AAKSHANK , central India's mega and fantabulous event is back again with, more plus extra, electrifying and vivifying events.

Our fest covers diverse events ranging from competitions like Dance, Drama, Fashion show, Singing to Star nights, DJ Nights, Informals and Stuntmania.

Till now AAKSHANK had the opportunity of being a platform for great stars and performers of the country like
Papon, Nkhil D'souza, Mohammed Irfan, Nikhil Chinappa, DJ Willy, Progressive Brothers
and the list can go on and on.

Having blessed to be a part of the distinguished cultural bonanza, we, students of IET invite you all to our event.
Join us and together we'll make AAKSHANK the most gratifying and memorable 4 days of the year 2019.
